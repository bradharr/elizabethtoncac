About Us:

Elizabethton Counseling and Arts Center opened their doors in October, 2018. ECAC provides individual and group therapy options that use treatment goals that are client-focused and centered on the individual experience to bring growth and transformation to the individual. Using empirically evidenced therapy, we work to help the client understand their past, focus on the present, and continue towards the future. ECAC offers a variety of experiences and opportunities for any age.

Elizabethton Counseling and Arts Center takes an eclectic approach with an existential focus.

Individual Therapy:

  • A warm, safe environment providing autonomy and empathetic care.
  • Confidentiality and emotional safety.
  • Treatment from a licensed professional counselor (LPC) and mental health service provider (MHSP)
  • Someone to join you in the journey through difficult decisions and times in your life.
  • Movement at your pace.
  • Decrease in negative emotions and behaviors with an increase in positive emotions and behaviors.

Group Therapy:

  • See, Listen, Play and create through your preferred means of expression.
  • Healing through the creative process.
  • Personal growth in a safe, supportive environment.
  • Introspection through self-awareness, self-understanding, and insight.
  • A safe and supportive environment facilitated by licensed therapists who are genuine, warm, empathetic, open, honest, congruent, and caring.
