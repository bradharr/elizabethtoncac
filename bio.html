Brandon M. Cole, LPC, MHSP

is a Tennessee state-licensed professional counselor and mental health service provider that has been in private practice for the past 5 years. Brandon has a background working with various mental health needs in children, teens, and adults and has worked with teenagers for over 20 years. This past year, Brandon moved his practice from Bristol to Elizabethton to expand services and increase a focus on group therapy in the area.

Brandon strongly believes you should feel comfortable with the therapist you choose and hopeful about the therapy you receive. Engagement and alignment are the cornerstone of a thriving therapeutic process and you, the client, are the foundation and priority.
